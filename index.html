Welcome to Force Ten Power Cleaning

We are a locally based Company operated by local people who understand your needs.
We undertake all Commercial and Domestic exterior cleaning.
We provide a professional high quality service at an affordable price.

  • All work is carried out with the latest exterior power and soft cleaning equipment and will return your property to its former beauty.
  • Driveways, pathways, patios and decking, in fact any hard external surfaces, can benefit from the cleaning process.
  • Commercial car parks and entrances cleaned to keep your customer appeal.
  • We soft wash those areas that need extra care e.g. conservatory roofs, fascia, gutters and solar panels.
  • We use industrial jet wash machinery at 200 bar for 3000 PSI
  • Bio wash, mild detergent or pure water is used depending on the treatment required.
  • Long reach water fed poles are used so that no scaffolding or plant is required.
